About Ticket Fix Me


In 2016, amidst all the software and sites available to the world, we identified a serious gap: nothing existed that would adequately handle maintenance requests for a small to medium sized business. Ticket systems do exist but they are either enterprise level tools or generic, free tools. Enterprise systems are incredibly costly and complex. Free, generic ticketing systems are not specific enough to effectively manage a specific problem, which leads to customization and therefore complexity. Ticketfixme is our answer. It provides a centralized website for tenants, managers, owners, and contractors to communicate from the beginning to the end of a maintenance request.

This is positive for all parties:
• The tenant has a straightforward, detailed site to log their requests.
• Management can monitor these requests, issue them to a handyman or contractor through a biddingprocess, and communicate with the tenant until the process is complete. Management can also scale the business by building a network of contractors. That is, if there is one request or hundreds, management can rely on a potentially limitless network rather than a static number of employees.
• The handyman/contractor can build a rapport with a business while building his own business.

All the while, each stage of the process is in plain view to all. We are excited to provide this valuable service to all varieties of rental housing as well as handymen and contractors. Pricing information for housing entities is available on request. ​
